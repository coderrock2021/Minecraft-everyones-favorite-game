how to play minecraft survival mode/hardcore mode/adventure mode/spectator mode 1. Type in a seed in the world generator, put the mode to survival and check your pc controls for minecraft 2. punch wood and create a crafting table and a wooden pickaxe 3. find a hill and mine stone and coal for fuel and weapon upgrades 4, find wheat seeds and hunt down three sheep for a bed and find other sources of food 4. create a house and survive your first night 5. then survive yor second day and night, hunt down more animals cook more food, create plant and animal farms and mine iron and find new crafting recipes and survive as long as you can
